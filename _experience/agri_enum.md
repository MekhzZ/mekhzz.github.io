Census Bureau of Statistics, Nepal. | Apr 2022 - Jun 2022

<p class="text-justify">Conducted a comprehensive survey of rural households and agricultural holdings to collect critical data for national policy planning.</p>

- **Data Collection & Accuracy:** Gathered detailed information on land use, crop production, livestock, and agricultural inputs, ensuring high data integrity through rigorous validation.

- **Fieldwork Management:** Organized and managed survey operations within a designated geographic area, optimizing time management to meet strict project deadlines.

- **Communication & Rapport:** Conducted one-on-one interviews with farmers, explaining census objectives to facilitate accurate and complete responses.

- **Problem-Solving:** Resolved operational challenges, including navigating to remote locations and clarifying technical agricultural terms for respondents.
